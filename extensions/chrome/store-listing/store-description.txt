NAME:
Shortlist — Job Importer

SHORT DESCRIPTION (132 chars max):
Import job listings from any job board into your Shortlist feed with one click. Works on LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Indeed, and more.

DETAILED DESCRIPTION:
Shortlist Job Importer lets you save job listings from any website directly into your Shortlist account while you browse.

How it works:
1. Browse to any job listing on LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Indeed, or any other job board
2. Click the Shortlist icon in your toolbar
3. The extension extracts the job details automatically using AI
4. Review the extracted title, company, and location
5. Click "Import to Shortlist" to add it to your feed

The job appears in your Shortlist dashboard with full details and gets automatically scored against your search criteria.

Features:
- Works on any* job board — LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Lever, Indeed, Glassdoor, corporate careers pages, and more
- One-click import with automatic field extraction
- AI-powered extraction handles any page layout
- Choose which search profile to import into
- Duplicate detection prevents re-importing the same listing
- "Edit in Shortlist" option for manual review before importing
- Recent imports history in the popup
- Dark mode support (follows your OS preference)

*Some job boards are more complex than others. We are continually working to improve extraction. In the case of a failure, users can always import manually, and we will analyze the error to improve our future extractions.

Requirements:
- A Shortlist account at https://shortlist.johnmoorman.com
- Must be signed into Shortlist in your browser
